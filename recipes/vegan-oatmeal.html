Vegan Oatmeal

Description

Easy to make, delicious to eat. Simple, fast and perfect to take out.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Vegan milk of choice
  • Chia seeds
  • Agave syrup
  • Peanut butter

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Let it rest in the fridge for a minimun of 8 hours.
  3. Enjoy!
